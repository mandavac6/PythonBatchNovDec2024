Tom Brady: The GOAT of the NFL
Full Name: Thomas Edward Patrick Brady Jr.
Born: August 3, 1977, in San Mateo, California, USA
Height/Weight: 6'4" (1.93 m), 225 lbs (102 kg)
Nickname: The GOAT (Greatest of All Time)

Career Highlights
NFL Teams:

New England Patriots (2000–2019): Drafted in the 6th round (199th overall) of the 2000 NFL Draft.
Tampa Bay Buccaneers (2020–2022): Signed as a free agent and led the team to a Super Bowl victory in his first season.
Super Bowl Wins:

7 Championships: (6 with the Patriots, 1 with the Buccaneers)
Most Super Bowl wins by any player in NFL history.
Super Bowl MVPs:

5 Awards: Recognized for his performances on the game's biggest stage.
Career Statistics:

Passing Yards: 89,214 (Regular Season - Most in NFL history).
Touchdowns: 649 (Regular Season - Most in NFL history).
Games Played: 335.
Accolades
NFL MVP Awards: 3 (2007, 2010, 2017).
Pro Bowls: 15 appearances (an all-time record).
All-Pro Selections: 6 times.
NFL All-Time Leader: In passing yards, touchdowns, and completions.
Why is He Considered the GOAT?
Longevity & Consistency: Played 23 NFL seasons at an elite level.
Clutch Performances: Known for leading game-winning drives, including the legendary comeback in Super Bowl LI (Patriots vs. Falcons).
Leadership: Elevated teammates' performances, whether rookies or veterans.
Adaptability: Dominated in two different teams, excelling even after leaving the Patriots system.
Life Beyond Football
Business Ventures:

Co-founded TB12 Sports, focusing on fitness and wellness.
Brady Brand: A clothing line launched in 2022.
Involved in multiple endorsements and media projects.
Media Career: Signed a multi-year broadcasting deal with Fox Sports to become an NFL analyst post-retirement.

Personal Life:

Married to supermodel Gisele Bündchen from 2009 to 2022 (divorced).
Father to three children.
Fun Facts
The Comeback Kid: Brady has 43 career game-winning drives, including playoffs.
Draft Underdog: Was the 199th pick in the 2000 NFL Draft, often referred to as "The Brady 6."
Diet & Fitness: Known for the TB12 Method, focusing on pliability, hydration, and a strict diet.
